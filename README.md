BioBizz Calculator

A small command-line tool to calculate the right amount of BioBizz nutrients for your plants.

The calculator follows the official BioBizz All Mix nutrient schedule. 
I got tired of looking at the feeding chart every time, so I made this little tool instead.

Just enter:

the amount of water (in liters) 
the current week of your plant

The calculator will show you exactly how much of each nutrient you need for that feeding.

Example:

How much water in liters? 

Water: 1

In which week is your plant? 

Week: 1

You need for Week 1 and 1 Liter of water:

Root Juice: 4 ml

Bio Heaven: 2 ml 

Acti Vera: 2 ml 

CalMag: 0.3 ml 

Features:

Simple command-line application 
Calculates nutrient amounts based on liters of water 
Uses the BioBizz All Mix feeding schedule 
Fast and easy to use
